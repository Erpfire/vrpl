About VRPL

Pioneering Waste-to-Energy Solutions for India

Who We Are

Varanasi Recyclers Private Limited

This Start Up company was formed with the objective of transforming all types of city wastes into Electricity & later into Green Hydrogen.

VRPL is a government-recognized startup (DPIIT certified) dedicated to solving India's waste management crisis through advanced plasma gasification technology. We convert Municipal Solid Waste, plastic waste, biomedical waste, and hazardous waste into clean syngas, which is then used to generate electricity and, in Phase 2, green hydrogen.

Our technology partnership with CSIR-CMERI and BARC ensures we're implementing indigenous (Swadeshi) technology that's proven, safe, and effective.

Parallely we are approaching schools and colleges for awareness programs, training & mentorship programs in the above domains in the form of capacity building under the State Govt. mission of creating Samriddh Uttar Pradesh-2047 & also Nari Shakti Mission-2047.

Mission of the Project

Viksit Bharat Mission-2047

Contributing to the PM initiative for developed India, through hiring all along 50% women employees in our project to make 70% women in economic activity to promote gender equality and economic empowerment.

SDG-3: Good Health and Well-being

The project will contribute to SDG-3 promoting awareness and learning for environment and other sustainability domains, ensuring healthier communities through cleaner air and reduced pollution.

SDG-6: Clean Water and Sanitation

This Plasma based initiative will prevent water & soil pollution through waste to energy conversion while having almost zero harmful emissions. No leachate will contaminate the ground water and this will ensure sanitation with MSW recycling.

Swadeshi Mantra: Indigenous Technology

This project has focused on the 'Swadeshi Mantra' while having the Indigenous technology developed by CSIR-CMERI which will make waste to energy and green hydrogen mission possible in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh.

Our Goals

Objective of the Project

Primary Objective

Scientific disposal of Municipal Solid Waste (MSW) / Plastic Waste / Biomedical waste (BMW) / Hazardous Waste (HW) / Metallic Waste of Varanasi city.

Technology Deployment

To deploy suitable technology developed by CSIR-CMERI in coordination with Positronics Innovation Private Limited, Durgapur, West Bengal and Bhabha Atomic Research Centre BARC, Mumbai for plasma Gasification/Pyrolysis of MSW/BMW/PW/HW of capacity 5TPD /10 TPD.
