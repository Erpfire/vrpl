Skill Development

Workshops & Training

Plasma Gasification Technology

Deep dive into the science and operations of plasma systems, covering thermal dynamics, syngas production, energy conversion efficiency, and safety protocols. Participants gain hands-on understanding of fourth-state-of-matter technology and its applications.

Duration: 2 days

Waste Management Best Practices

Comprehensive overview of modern waste handling, segregation techniques, processing methods, and circular economy principles. Learn about waste-to-energy pathways, environmental compliance, and sustainable waste management strategies aligned with government regulations.

Duration: 1 day

Green Hydrogen Production

Future-focused training on hydrogen economy fundamentals, production methods from waste-derived syngas, storage technologies, and applications. Aligned with India's Green Hydrogen Mission and 5 MMT capacity targets by 2030.

Duration: 1 day

Environmental Compliance & Monitoring

Understanding regulations, certifications, and monitoring requirements including CPCB guidelines, CEMS implementation, emission standards, and Life-Cycle Analysis methodologies. Learn to navigate regulatory frameworks and maintain compliance.

Duration: 1 day

Women in Clean Energy Leadership

Specialized program aligned with Nari Shakti Mission 2047, empowering women through technical training, leadership development, and career pathways in renewable energy sector. Supporting our commitment to 50% women workforce.

Duration: 2 days
Partnership

CSIR-CMERI Guidelines

All our training programs follow CSIR-CMERI (Council of Scientific and Industrial Research - Central Mechanical Engineering Research Institute) guidelines to ensure technical excellence and safety.

We're hiring skilled manpower trained according to these rigorous standards, ensuring operational safety, efficiency, and alignment with indigenous technology development goals. Our partnership with CSIR-CMERI and BARC guarantees that every team member receives world-class technical education.


Training certification recognized by CSIR-CMERI validates technical competency in plasma gasification operations, safety protocols, and waste management systems.
